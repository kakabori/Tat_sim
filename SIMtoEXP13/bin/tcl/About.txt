Program 'SIMtoEXP' (originally 'VolProb') is for converting SIMulated data to
scattering form factors and comparing those to experimental data.
It also calculates the Volume Probability Distributions
according to Horia's paper [Petrache et al. BJ 72, 2237-2242 (1997)].
